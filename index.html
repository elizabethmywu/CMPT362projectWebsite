AniMo

A buddy to cheer you on

AniMo combines a virtual pet simulation with a focus app to help users enhance their productivity and time management. Pets provide users with sweet encouragement and gentle motivation to help them achieve their goals and finish their tasks.

Made by Group 27 of the Fall 2024 offering of CMPT362.
